# User Painpoints

## Painpoint: Confusing Cycle Tracking Apps
Many apps overload users with predictions, icons, fertility terms, and unclear calendar states.

MyStree Soul solves this by using plain language, simple cycle views, and readable summaries that focus on what the user can understand today.

## Painpoint: Too Much Medical Jargon
Users may feel excluded or anxious when health apps use clinical terms without explanation.

MyStree Soul solves this by explaining health terms simply and keeping insights short, gentle, and actionable.

## Painpoint: Fear-Based Symptom Checkers
Symptom tools can make users feel scared, judged, or convinced something is wrong.

MyStree Soul solves this by avoiding diagnosis, using calm wording, and encouraging professional care only in supportive, appropriate ways.

## Painpoint: Forgetting Symptom History Before Doctor Visits
Users often forget when symptoms happened, how often they occurred, or what else was happening in their cycle.

MyStree Soul solves this by helping users log symptoms, mood, sleep, cycle timing, and notes in one place so context is easier to review.

## Painpoint: Difficulty Connecting Symptoms, Mood, Sleep, And Cycle
Users may notice patterns but struggle to connect them across days or weeks.

MyStree Soul solves this by organizing logs around cycle timing and showing simple pattern summaries without overclaiming.

## Painpoint: Scattered Health Records
Reports, prescriptions, scan results, and doctor notes may live across messages, folders, photos, and paper.

MyStree Soul solves this with Vault, a private record area for uploads, notes, and organized health context.

## Painpoint: Lack Of Emotional Support
Health tracking can feel lonely, especially when symptoms are recurring or hard to explain.

MyStree Soul solves this with Bloop, a gentle companion for quick logs, reminders, and reassuring context.

## Painpoint: Apps Feeling Cold Or Clinical
Users may avoid apps that feel sterile, judgmental, or designed around data instead of people.

MyStree Soul solves this with warm colors, soft cards, supportive language, and a calm mobile-first experience.

## Painpoint: Users Not Knowing What To Tell A Doctor
Users may not know which details matter during an appointment.

MyStree Soul solves this by preserving symptom history, cycle timing, notes, and record context so users can share clearer observations.
